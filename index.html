Quisn — Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to Quisn mobile apps and related services (“Quisn”, “we”, “our”, “us”). If you have questions, contact us at michael@quisn.com.

1) Overview

We collect and use information to provide core functionality like accounts, messaging, notifications, and content sharing (e.g., posts and photos). We do not sell personal data.

2) Camera Permission Disclosure (Android)

Why we request android.permission.CAMERA: Quisn uses the camera only when you choose to capture photos or videos—for example, to update a profile picture; attach images/videos to posts or chat; or showcase meals, menus, or services.

  • Capture happens on demand (never in the background).
  • You can cancel before uploading.
  • If you upload, the media is sent to our servers to deliver the feature you requested (e.g., show content in feeds or messages).
  • You can delete uploaded content where supported, or request deletion (see Section 10).

We do not use the camera for biometric identification, covert recording, or continuous/background capture.

3) Information We Collect

  • Account & Profile: name, email/phone, username, role(s), profile details you provide.
  • Content You Create: photos/videos, posts, comments, messages, and related metadata.
  • Device & App Data: device model, OS version, app version, language, diagnostics.
  • Usage Data: feature interactions, in-app events (e.g., likes), presence/online status, session timestamps.
  • Network Data: IP address, coarse location inferred from IP (not precise GPS unless explicitly granted elsewhere).
  • Notifications Data: push tokens (e.g., FCM) and delivery outcomes.
  • Log Data: server logs for security/troubleshooting.
  • Authentication Data: access/refresh tokens and identifiers to keep you signed in securely.

4) How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the app (accounts, feeds, chat, orders/updates, discovery).
  • Enable messaging and real-time features (presence, typing indicators, read receipts).
  • Send notifications you enable (activity updates, messages, promotions if opted-in).
  • Prevent fraud, secure accounts, and enforce policies.
  • Analyze performance and reliability (crash reports, diagnostics).

5) Push Notifications

We use device tokens (e.g., Firebase Cloud Messaging) to deliver notifications you allow. Tokens are stored with your account and can be revoked by logging out, disabling notifications in-app/OS, or requesting deletion.

6) Real-Time Features

We use secure web sockets and similar tech for events like presence updates, typing indicators, and instant message delivery. These transmit only what’s needed (e.g., user ID, event type, timestamps).

7) Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service Providers: hosting, databases, push delivery (e.g., FCM), logging/monitoring, analytics, support tools.
  • Legal & Safety: to comply with legal requests or protect rights, safety, or security.
  • Business Transfers: in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, data may transfer as part of the transaction.

We do not sell your personal information.

8) Data Retention

  • Account/profile data: kept while your account is active.
  • User content: kept until you delete it or your account, subject to backups/legal obligations.
  • Push tokens: kept while notifications are enabled or until invalid/removed.
  • Logs/diagnostics: retained only as long as necessary for security and troubleshooting.

9) Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the data (e.g., encrypted transport, access controls). No system is perfectly secure; please use a strong password and keep your device updated.

10) Your Choices & Rights

  • Access/Update: edit your profile and settings in-app.
  • Notifications: manage in-app preferences and OS-level permissions.
  • Camera & Media: you control capture/upload; delete your content where supported.
  • Deletion: request deletion of your account/data at michael@quisn.com.
  • Regional Rights: where applicable (e.g., GDPR/CCPA), you may access, correct, delete, port, or restrict processing.

11) Children’s Privacy

Quisn is not directed to children under the age where parental consent is required by local law. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us.

12) International Transfers

We may process and store information in countries different from where you reside, with appropriate safeguards.

13) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. We’ll change the “Last updated” date and, when required, notify you in-app or by other means. Continued use means you accept the update.
